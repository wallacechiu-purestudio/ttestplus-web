Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1: Why can't I click the "Upload CSV" button?

A: Some advanced features, including data uploading, require the Pro version of the App. Clicking these restricted features will prompt you to upgrade.

Q2: Do I need to pay again if I switch devices?

A: No! T-test Plus supports Universal Purchase. Simply click "Restore Purchases" on the paywall screen to regain access to your Pro features for free.

Q3: Does the App upload my research data to the cloud?

A: Absolutely not. All your data entries and charts are processed strictly locally on your device. We do not collect or upload any of your information.

常見問題解答 (FAQ)

Q1: 為什麼我無法點擊「上傳資料 (Upload CSV)」？

A: 本應用程式的部分進階功能需要升級為 Pro 版本。點擊受限功能時會自動彈出升級視窗。

Q2: 我換了裝置（如 Mac 換到 iPad），需要重新購買嗎？

A: 完全不需要！本應用程式支援通用購買。請在升級畫面點選「恢復購買 (Restore Purchases)」，即可免費找回您的權限。

Q3: 應用程式會把我的研究數據上傳到伺服器嗎？

A: 絕對不會。所有計算都在您的裝置本地端完成。我們不會收集或上傳您的任何資料，100% 保障您的隱私。
